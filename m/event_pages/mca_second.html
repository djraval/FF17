ImaJigsaw

Showing image parts in progressive manner and participants have to identify as fast as possible.

Round Details:

Round - 1
Participants will be shown image related to the field of computer science, like, any hardware component, logo or well-known personality and they have to identify it. Correct Answer will give you 1 Point. The predefined amount of time will be given for each image.

Round - 2
In the round 2, all qualified participants will be shown the Partial images. In some cases, Full image will be provided part by part. Each participant has to raise their Given Symbolic Number. Correct Answer will give you 2 Points.

Rules:
1. Participant has to raise the symbolic number to answer.
2. Participant who raise first will be given a chance to answer.
3. If tie, participants qualified to answer personally.
4. Partial answer will not be considered.
5. In Case of any discrepancy, Event Coordinators decision will be taken as final.
