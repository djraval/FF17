Time attack

STAGE 1: Two teams, each team consist of 4 players, will be taken up for battle round which serves as elimination round. In this battle round two teams would compete and face each other in a setup arena for 3 minutes, Here each player would be provided a water gun along with a tissue attached to the cap and an carton armor having tissue paper attached in center . The task would be to tear the tissues of players in opposite team and accordingly points will get accounted in that team. Arena is divided into two parts (Favorable area — with objects where one can hide and unfavorable area- with no object for hiding), one who wins the toss can select favorable area. The winning team would be taken up to next stage. NOTE: Boys team will compete with boys team only and girls team will compete with girls team only STAGE 2: From now onwards they compete with one another against time (team is now divided in individuals: 4 players). This stage consist of 6 levels, Each level is having a task which needs to get completed in 1 Minute. Player is provided 2 lives (2 chances to attempt a task) after completion of level 1. Level 1 is common for all 4 players named "Pick It Up", from here only winner levels up and move for next level/s. Each task, on completion, will make player earn cash which will increase by 10 Rupees per level. A checkpoint is provided after completing Level 2 successfully, where Player is asked to quit with earned price or continue playing; if continued and failed at any above level, player loses earned cash. The one who clears all 6 levels will be declared as Winner of "TimeAttack" and will cash out with earned price.
