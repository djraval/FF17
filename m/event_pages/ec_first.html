Buzz it to Quiz it

Event based on basic Electrical and Electronic concepts and mental ability along with testing of circuit designing and equipment usage

Round 1 (Qualifier Round): Circuit Implementation
Each team has to implement a buzzer circuit on breadboard as per the manual provided on the spot. Teams successfully completing this task in minimum duration with at most accuracy will be selected for the next round.
It will also include a surprise bumper round based on the teamsâ€™ performance and risk taking ability.

Round 2 (Final Round): Quiz
This round consists of Basic Technical and calculative Quiz. Buzzer made by the qualifying teams of previous round will be made into use for this round. Winning Teams will be selected as per the scores acquired and final judgment of authorities. This round will be abide by time constraint and negative points. Bonus points will be provided for every passed question answered.
In case of clashes like multiple teams hitting the buzzers at same instant, their respective answers shall be collected without disclosure, and shall be compared with the correct answers. The round may also implement the tie breaker whenever needed.

Rules:
1. Students must bring DMM, Breadboard Connecting wires, etc.
2. Necessary components ie. Resistors, capacitors and lab access shall be provided by the organizers.
3. A team must consist of members not more than 2.
4. Teams not conducting the basic ethics and harming the competitiveness are subject to immediate disqualification.
