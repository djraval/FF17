Round 1: - Hometown round

International or Club gameplay will be decided on the basis of a toss. Both players will be allowed to select a team and then that player will have to in his own team in this round also known as Home Team. Round comprises of 4 min half that is complete 8 min game.
This round will be of one game.
Approx Time: - 10 Min.
Round 2: - Away round

The structure of the competition is tree structure that is each pair of round will be knock out round. The finale will be of around 20 min full having same round structure.
